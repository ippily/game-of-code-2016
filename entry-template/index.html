NutriKnow

An app to search for nutritional products close to you

NutriKnow

Brought to you by Emily Ip

What is this app about?

  • It allows users to search for and view info on health products
  • They can compare prices of these products
  • Find the closest store that stocks them
  • Or buy them online
  • Users won't have to navigate to different websites and instead can search within the app

These products can include health and sports nutrition products such as nut butters, protein powders, different milks, nuts, seeds, snacks etc

Who is the target audience?

  • People who seek to buy nutritional products either close to their home or online and get them delivered to their house
  • Health conscious individuals
  • Individuals who seek to buy these products at their convenience
  • Someone who wants to try new products or compare with others

Features

1. Search for products
2. View product information
3. Rate and review products
4. Location services
5. Favourite products
6. Promo codes
7. Monthly Subscriptions
